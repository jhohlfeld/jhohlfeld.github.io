How we do it

1. Listen
First we listen to what our clients need. Are they missing something? Are they put on hold or restrained to grow?
2. Analyse
Then we talk to our network of managers, designers, strategists, coaches, developers, ... if there might already be a solution.
3. Architect
We build a team, create a concept through interviewing and research, hire staff and execute the project together with our client.
4. Learn
Finally we try and iterate over the results, looking into ways of simplifying things for the future, put some scalability and reproduceability into the process.

Our ultimate goal is to create long lasting partnerships, find new markets, create spin-offs and invent new products for the b2b and b2c markets.

What is Polyban?

Polyban is a collaborative of technical people and managers. We focus on important things: doing what we love while creating a sustainable way of life.

„Do what you're best at and improve yourself — while getting things done.‟

We want to break isolation, facilitate change and help others to get along. That's why we chose to work as a cooperative. We have all ingredients — so let's do it!

We love doing things

Our main concern is the management and execution of projects related to web technologies.

However, we are a group of people with all sorts of interests — managers, designers, scientists, programmers.

We look beyond what's immediatly ahead. And because we are independent, we are able to develop new things, walk new ways without worrying about whether it's appropriate or not.
